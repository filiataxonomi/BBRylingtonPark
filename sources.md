## The audited Annual Reports

The reserve balances, the 2020 handover (Note 25/26 of the 2019/20 report) and the livestock counts on the [Financials page](financials.html) come from the audited Annual Reports for 2019/20 through 2024/25, all published in the [Shire's minutes and agendas section](https://www.boyupbrook.wa.gov.au/council/your-council/minutes-agendas.aspx). The 2019/20 report is [Shire document 952](https://www.boyupbrook.wa.gov.au/documents/952/attachment-28-july-2021); the 2024/25 report is [Shire document 1453](https://www.boyupbrook.wa.gov.au/documents/1453/annual-report-20242025).
