How to send a PayID payment

  1. Open your banking app and choose Pay or Pay Someone.
  2. Select PayID and choose Email as the PayID type.
  3. Enter the PayID email shown above — easiest is to tap the address to copy it, then paste it in.
  4. Check that the name shown comes up as C Wilson before you confirm.
  5. Enter any amount and send. Payments usually arrive in under a minute.
